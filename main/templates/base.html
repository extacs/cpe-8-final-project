services

THREE SERVICES.
TWO OFFICES.
ONE HUB.

LSPU NavGO currently serves two core campus offices, with more on the way. By allowing students to handle preliminary tasks online, we aim to eliminate the uncertainty and long lines that often might cause frustration. Through these services, we do not wish to eliminate on-site physical interactions, but rather to bridge the gap between digital convenience and on-campus necessities.

SERVICE 01

BAO — BUSINESS AFFAIRS OFFICE

Order uniforms, check stock availability, and pick up your items without ever having to line up just to ask if your size is in stock.

  • Real-time inventory with restock dates
  • Cart and checkout flow
  • Printable claim form on confirmation
  • Order history and reprint anytime
SERVICE 02

CLINIC — HEALTH SERVICES

Schedule physical check-ups or request medical records using a calendar and walk in with your appointment slip already printed.

  • Calendar-based appointment scheduling
  • Check-up or medical records selection
  • Printable appointment slip generation
  • Staff forecast dashboard for volume prep
