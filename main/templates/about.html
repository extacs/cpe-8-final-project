THE PROJECT

WHAT IS
LSPU NAVGO?

NavGO is a web-application designed to make school transactions and services much more accessible and easier to process online, reducing the need to check for onsite follow-ups. It provides access for both students and certain staff (BAO and Clinic). Some of the main features included in the web-app are Registration, Login, Staff Dashboard, Student Dashboard, and a Student Profile for storing user information like name, ID, and program.

The web application features BAO Management for handling student orders, an Appointment System for clinic scheduling and medical requests, and a Student Center where students can buy and sell items. It includes a quick access web view for easier navigation. The Student records such as order history and appointment data will be stored on the system. LSPU NAVGO aims to improve access to school services through a platform that helps students complete transactions more easily, improving overall convenience and efficiency for both students and staff.

🏪

STUDENT CENTER

Provides a place for students to buy-and-sell their products without requiring them to pay for any kinds fees. For certain colleges, if their department is currently having an event they could just list their items for a limited time (but with coordination with certain staffs that handle the web-app)

⚡

QUICK ACCESS WEB

Currently, our webapp offers only a single path for accessing and using our services since the other path is currently in works by our team. This quick web view foreshadows the services that will appear inside the gamified top-down version. The team is doing their best to provide the best experience for students.

🛒

BUSINESS AFFAIRS OFFICE SERVICE

An eCommerce type of interface but requires no any kinds of fees. Students can check if their needs are available currently on BAO, without having to visit every now and then. Students may also see the possible date an item would be available. After placing an order, the student will be given a form (by the system) that they'll present on the counter at the BAO. Which speeds up the process.

🏥

CLINIC SERVICE

Students can appoint for physical check-ups or processing medical records by their set-date, but by "appointing" they will only be given an appointment form that they'll bring with upon attending the clinic, but will still require the student to scan their filled appointment form and submit upon appointment.

OUR MISSION

WHY WE
BUILT THIS

We built LSPU NAVGO to solve the common challenges students face, such as limited transportation, manual processes, and the need to physically visit the campus just to check item availability or book appointments. This web application brings school transactions online, significantly improving accessibility, convenience, and efficiency for both students and staff[cite: 1].

🗺️

EASE OF ACCESS

We created a centralized hub where users can easily navigate to key services like the BAO, Clinic, and Student Center. This design drastically reduces the need for walk-in visits and long waiting times.

⚡

ZERO HIDDEN FRICTION

Auto-populated forms, printable slips, real-time stock. Every feature is designed to save time, reduce manual workloads, and improve overall efficiency in handling requests.

🤝

STUDENT-FIRST DESIGN

The platform was built specifically for university students to make completing transactions as easy as possible. As also being a students helps us correlate with the student's micro struggles inside the university.

LOOKING AHEAD

We are continuously working to improve LSPU NAVGO. Our roadmap includes:

1. Multi-Language Support- Allows users to select from a variety of multiple language options to improve accessibility and usability.

2. Mobile Responsiveness - A mobile-friendly version of the web application to ensure better access for smartphones and tablets.

3. Cross-Platform Compatibility - Further development should ensure consistent performance across various devices, browsers, and screen sizes.

4. Pixel Campus Navigation - Allows users to easily locate buildings, offices, and facilities in the LSPU through navigating a pixel map version of the school.

5. ChatBot - An AI-powered chatbot that assists users in navigating the web application, answering frequently asked questions, and guiding them through the system.

6. Security Enhancements - Includes stronger security measures such as user authentication upgrades, data encryption, and protection against common web vulnerabilities.
